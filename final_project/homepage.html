A Beginner's Guide to Electronic Music: By Nicholas Hughes

I've loved listening to electronic music for a long time now. Electronic dance music, or "EDM," has proliferated itself into many areas of everyday life like commercials and social media, but still many people have no idea just how broad of a type of music it is. This is your one-stop shop for beginning to understand the differences between a handful of electronic genres. Below, you will find links to a brief description of each genre along with a few songs handpicked by me as a genre showcase.
